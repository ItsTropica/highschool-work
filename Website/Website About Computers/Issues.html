Social Context of Software Design

There are quite a bit of things to know about software design, so we chose the three we think are best to know:

Ergonomics

Ergonomics is the science behind the design of equipment for people of the world to have ease of access while using the equipment. Things to consider when designing equipment include; physical, psychological needs, and work requirements. Ergonomically designed equipment helps with staying comfortable, feeling satisfied with the product, staying productive and is healthy to the people who use it.

Inclusivity

Inclusivity is the thought of the design for the people. The purpose of the design needs to be considered to a wide variety of people and their needs and must take into account the many different types of different backgrounds that people bring to the computer world. Some perspectives that need to be considered and included are the cultural, economical, gender, social and disabled side of the community.

Privacy

Privacy is the protection behind whatever you do on any website, this protection includes; password protection, ip protection, credit card information and all the other information you give to the website. The website's privacy policy protects you from the information being sold to scammers in Kolkata who will then call you and try and scam you for money. The only way your information from a website with a privacy policy can be leaked is by being hacked.
