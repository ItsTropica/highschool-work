What is this website?

This website is about Computer and Software and how they work and how they've changed our lives forever. The navigation above will show you around the site and go in-depth about the topics; social & ethical issues, elements of a computer system, and the relationship between hardware & software.

Why is this important?

This topic is important for anyone who would want to know a single thing about computers; whether its to navigate the web, build their own pc, or just to open solitaire, there will always be someone who's curious as to how it even works. It is good to know these things as it could help you later if you ever choose a subject/job that requires any of this information.
